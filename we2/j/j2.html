Unusual places in Jaipur

Amer Sagar

If you are looking for best offbeat things to do in Jaipur then you can visit Amer Sagar. This is a 17th century lake that supplied water to Amer and Jaigarh forts.

This is situated near Amer Fort between Kheri Gate and the Anokhi Museum. The best time to visit this place is during monsoon season as the weather is relaxed and you get amazing views.
