Unusual places in Jaipur

Chandlai Lake

A beautiful lake visited by migratory birds in winter, this is one of the hidden places to visit in Jaipur. Bird watchers and explorers love the beauty of Chandlai Lake.

Chandlai Lake is on the Tonk Road on Kota-Jaipur Highway. Located on the outskirts of the city a trip to this lake will be an unique experience in Jaipur. You can also view the rustic lifestyle of Jaipur here.
